Symptoms

Everyone have different symptoms of breast cancer. Some people do not have any signs or symptoms at all. Some warning signs of breast cancer are:

  1. New lump in the breast or underarm (armpit)
  2. Thickening or swelling of part of the breast
  3. Irritation or dimpling of breast skin
  4. Redness or flaky skin in the nipple area or the breast
  5. Nipple discharge other than breast milk, including blood
  6. Pain in any area of the breast
  7. Any change in the size or the shape of the breast
Please keep in mind that these symptoms can happen with other conditions that are not cancer. If you have any signs or symptoms that worry you, be sure to see your doctor right away.

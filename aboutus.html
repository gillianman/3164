Predict Breast Cancer

Predict Breast Cancer is an online tool that helps medical professions detect early stages of breast cancer via mammography.

Start Predict

Overview

Predict breast cancer is a web-based interface that can advise clients of early stages of breast cancer using mammography.

Our predictive model aims to uplift the current standard of early stage breast cancer detection through the use of deep learning to assist medical professionals make more informed decisions in treating potential patients with cancer.

Who is it for?

Predict breast cancer is designed for medical professions such as doctors and radiologists.

As our model is dedicated to medical professionals, please contact us for login details to begin your journey with us.

How does it work?

Our predictive model is a machine learning model that combines both classical machine learning and deep learning techniques, specifically Conventional Neural Network (CNN), to develop an accurate predict model for early breast cancer detections.
